Account Deletion

Prospector's Helper

Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, email:

support@solixardigital.com

Include in Your Request

  • Your registered email address
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and profile
  • All markers you've created
  • All photos you've uploaded
  • Friend connections
  • Subscription data (handled separately by app stores)

Processing Time

Your request will be processed within 30 days.

App Store Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions made through Apple App Store or Google Play are managed through their respective subscription settings. To cancel a subscription:

  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions
